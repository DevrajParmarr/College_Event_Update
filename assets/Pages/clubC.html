NAME OF THE CLUB:GS PRODUCTION HOUSE ABOUT CLUB:

GS Production House was established on November 25th, 2018, with the primary goal of recognizing, refining and promoting the talents of students of SGSITS, Indore across various creative spheres.

GS Production House is a media production club and the official supervisor of all the social media handles of SGSITS, Indore. We conduct various enthralling events and activities wherein the students can participate and exhibit their skills without any apprehension. We are a student run organisation which provides an intriguing platform for imaginative minds to enhance their creative abilities. GS Production House gives a powerful head start to new learners, transforming them into aspiring and driven individuals.
